HISTORY OF IT DEPARTMENT!

The IT department at Federal University Dutse (FUD) has evolved significantly since the university's establishment in 2011. Initially, the core IT functions were likely managed within a smaller unit, possibly under the Registrar's office or a general support services department. The Computer Science Department, which later became the Faculty of Computing, played a crucial role in developing the university's IT infrastructure and expertise
